The Aurora Process

Aurora is designed to be simple. No long onboarding, no complicated agency structures — just clear marketing support when you need it, the way you want it.

Step 1

You tell us the outcome you want to achieve

This might be launching a campaign, improving your website, refreshing brand messaging, or creating materials for a new service. A short conversation is usually enough to get started.

Step 2

We define what needs to be created or improved

The focus is always on what will actually help the business grow, not unnecessary strategy documents or long planning phases.

Step 3

Aurora moves straight into production

Websites, campaigns, creative assets, messaging, and digital platforms are designed and built quickly using human creativity supported by AI-powered production. Ideas move from concept to launch without unnecessary delays.

Step 4

Aurora supports one task, a short project, or ongoing marketing needs

Some businesses use Aurora occasionally when new initiatives arise. Others use it as an ongoing marketing capability without needing to build an internal marketing department or team.
